    dFrost is back!

    The annual design and cultural festival hosted by National Institute of Design, Bengaluru is all set to take you on an inter-dimensional tour of your imagination.

    This year dFROST explores the interdisciplinary realities of design and the convergence of these dimensions into ideas. We journey onto our creatives and delve into the unlimited possibilities that are made in our own realities. Learn, create, and explore experiences that manifest in our minds and materialise on-campus this year.

    NATIONAL INSTITUTE OF DESIGN

  • Schedule

    National Institute of Design is internationally acclaimed as one of the finest educational and research institutions in the field of design. The Business Week, USA has listed NID as one of the top 25 European & Asian design programmes in the world. NID was declared an ‘Institution of National Importance’ by the Act of Parliament, by virtue of the National Institute of Design Act, 2014.

    NID Bangalore, located in Peenya, one of the largest industrial zones in Asia, addresses the immediate need for an exclusive Design Research centre in the country, by fostering a creative design spirit, and sighting new opportunities and frontiers by leveraging NID's design acumen.

  • dTalks

    • Dhruv Saxena

      He heads the design practice at Uncommon - a digital product design consultancy that has helped many of India’s and SE Asia’s unicorn startups like Flipkart, Myntra and Swiggy, achieve rapid exponential growth through design.

    • Daniel Burka

      Daniel Burka is currently the director of product and design at Resolve to Save Lives, a global initiative to improve cardiovascular health in low- and middle-income countries.

    • Phani Tetali

      Phani Tetali is a professor at IDC, IIT Bombay with more than 18 years of experience in the Animation and Gaming industries. He is ex-vice president of FXLabs studios, Danlaw Technologies India Ltd and DQ entertainment. He is also the founder of Animatix Studios, Chennai and was a Member of the jury, FICCI Frames Best Animation Film Awards, 2008 and 2009. He plans to show us the same India we all live in. If it feels different, well it is a cartoonist’s perspective.

    • Abhinav Chhikara

      Abhinav is the Head of Design at Unacademy, a venture funded ed-tech startup. He is the author of Bloomsbury published Pyjama Profit and also runs a YouTube channel on building creative careers.

    • Anil Reddy

      Anil Reddy is Founder and Design Director at Lollypop studio, a UI/UX design firm based in Bangalore.. He has earlier worked with Ogilvy and TBWA. In 2017, he was awarded the prestigious Designer of the Year by the Times Network. His talk will be a reflection of his life and all the lessons budding designers can take away from it. A short “dos & don’ts” of how to make it in the world.

    Saptarshi Prakash - Motion Design in User Interfaces

    A problem solver at heart, Saptarshi Prakash leads the design of consumer products at Swiggy. Saptarshi’s 3-hour workshop will be a hands-on working session helping attendees getting started with the UX chreography, especially Motion Design in user interfaces, getting the UI elements alive in Adobe After Effects.

    Kumar Ahir - (AR)e you ready?

    With over 12 years of interaction design experience, Kumar Ahir is a noted AR/VR evangelist from India. After graduating from IDC and working with industry leaders like Microsoft and Symantec, He co-founded Oobi with a vision to make digital interactions tangible, natural and fun. Participants should prepare to get their hands dirty in this 3-hour workshop where they will be building an AR application from scratch on Unity.
